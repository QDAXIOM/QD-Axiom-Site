A Three-Phase Vision

QD Axiom is not just an insurance solution - it's a gateway into a broader privacy-first technology ecosystem.

Phase 1: Insurance Access

Our initial focus is democratizing access to insurance through data-driven technology. Using privacy-preserving wearable integration, we'll provide free or affordable insurance options to those traditionally underserved.

  • Privacy-first data collection
  • AI-powered risk assessment
  • Affordable coverage options
  • Support for underserved communities

Phase 2: Income Generation

Building on our foundation, we'll introduce tools for income generation and data custody, enabling users to monetize their own data on their terms while maintaining complete privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about QD Axiom and our approach

QD Axiom leverages anonymized data insights and partnerships with insurance providers to negotiate better rates. Through our innovative risk assessment model, we can offer free basic coverage to qualifying users while maintaining sustainable operations through premium tier options and data analytics services.

Our initial launch will focus on essential health coverage and income protection insurance specifically designed for gig workers, small business owners, and individuals with variable income. As we grow, we plan to expand into additional insurance categories based on community needs and feedback.

We employ end-to-end encryption, anonymization protocols, and decentralized storage to ensure your data remains private. Our system is designed so that only the minimum necessary information is used for insurance qualification, and you maintain complete control over what data is shared and when.

Our initial free insurance program will prioritize individuals without current coverage, particularly gig workers, small business owners, and those experiencing unemployment. Qualification is not based on income level but on need and participation in our data ecosystem. The first 15,000 users will receive priority access to our free coverage options.
